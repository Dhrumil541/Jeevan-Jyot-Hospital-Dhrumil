Maternity

One Of The Most Trusted & Recognised Maternity Hospital

Jeevan Jyot Hospital is situated at Navi Mumbai with a 10 bedded ICU. This Maternity and Multispeciality hospital is a well known centre for complete medical care.

The services provided are Laparoscopic & General surgery including all superspeciality branches and other specialities like Ophthalmology, Otorhinolaryngology, Dermatology, Pediatric, Psychiatry, Haematology, Oncology, Urology, Orthopaedic & Plastic Surgery.

All the medical superspecialities like Endocrinology, Neurology, Gastroenterology, Cardiology, Nephrology, Respiratory are also covered. Our doctors are experts in taking care of your health problems.

The hospital has a 24 Hour Pathology Lab and radiological services like 2D Echo, X-ray, Sonography, Colour Doppler, C-ARM. Maternity care: Recognised as one of the most trusted and recognized hospitals for maternal and child healthcare, Jeevan Jyot Hospital supports you throughout your pregnancy.

We are committed to providing accurate and excellent medical care to each patient. Our Critical Care Services meet the needs of patients facing an emergency health condition. By using advanced therapeutic, monitoring, and diagnostic technology, our experienced experts improve the patient condition such that their underlying injury or illness can then be treated.
